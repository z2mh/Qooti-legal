Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2025

Your privacy matters. This policy explains exactly what data Qooti collects, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected.

1. Who We Are

Qooti is a calorie and nutrition tracking app. When we say "Qooti," "we," "us," or "our," we mean the team behind the Qooti app. If you have questions about this policy, reach us on Instagram: @useqooti.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data needed to deliver the app's features:

CategoryDataPurpose
Account Name, email address Authentication and account management
Profile Age, gender, height, weight, activity level, dietary goal Calculating your personalized calorie and nutrition targets
Food Logs Meals, food names, portions, nutritional values, timestamps Tracking your daily intake and progress
Food Photos Images captured for AI food analysis Identifying food and estimating nutrition via AI
Barcode Scans Product barcodes Fetching nutritional information for packaged foods
Progress Photos Body photos you choose to capture Stored locally and/or in your account for your personal tracking
Weight Logs Body weight entries over time Tracking weight progress
Apple Health Steps, active energy, weight (if you grant permission) Enriching your nutrition and activity view
Device Device type, OS version, app version, crash logs Debugging and improving app stability

We do not collect payment information — all transactions are handled by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the Qooti app and its features
  • To calculate your calorie targets, macros, and nutritional summaries
  • To analyze food photos using AI and return nutritional estimates
  • To sync your data across devices through your account
  • To send you reminders and notifications (only with your permission)
  • To detect and fix bugs and improve app performance

We do not use your personal data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Qooti uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

ServicePurposeData Shared

OpenAI AI-powered food analysis Food photos and text descriptions (not linked to your identity)
RevenueCat Subscription management Anonymized purchase and subscription status
Apple App Store payments and Apple Health integration Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

All third-party providers are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes described above.

5. Food Photos & AI Analysis

When you use the AI food analysis feature, your photo is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. The photo is used solely to identify the food and estimate its nutritional content — it is not stored by OpenAI beyond the duration of the API request, and it is not linked to your personal identity.

Progress photos you take are stored securely in your account and are never shared with third parties.

6. Apple Health

Qooti may request access to Apple Health data (such as steps and body weight) to enhance your tracking experience. This integration is entirely optional. You control what data Qooti can read or write through your iPhone's Health app settings. We never share your Health data with any third party.

7. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Your account is protected by secure authentication
  • We regularly review our security practices and update them as needed

No system is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data associated with it is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked to you may be retained for analytical purposes.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate data through the app's profile settings
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all associated data directly from the app
  • Portability — Request an export of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on Instagram: @useqooti.

10. Children's Privacy

Qooti is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you through the app. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
